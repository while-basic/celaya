## The Orchestra's Secret

*[Voice lowers, speaking more intimately]*

You see, the problem with most multi-agent systems is that they break down in actual use. Either one agent dominates the conversation, or they all talk at once, or you get endless circular discussions.

We spent three years solving this problem. We created what we call the Three-Layer Round-Robin Orchestration system.

*[Voice becomes more animated, hands gesture to emphasize points]*

Layer One is a Baseline Scheduler – a classic round-robin with a "turn token" that ensures every agent gets fair airtime.

Layer Two is an Interrupt Protocol – when something urgent happens, like Sentinel detecting a security threat, it can raise its hand with a priority level and jump the line.

Layer Three is Arbitration & Recovery – handling conflicts, voting on disagreements, and ensuring the system is resilient to failure.

This is what lets our agents have a coherent conversation while still letting critical information jump the queue. No other multi-agent AI system has solved this problem as elegantly.

*[Walks across the stage, voice becoming passionate]*

But enough talking about it. Let me show you what it can do.
